SENATE BILL\t331

 

AN ACT\trelative to waivers of deductibles in automobile insurance policies.

 

SPONSORS:\tSen. Soucy, Dist 18; Sen. Cavanaugh, Dist 16

 

COMMITTEE:\tCommerce

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill requires any automobile insurance policy that contains coverage for physical damage by reason of collision to waive any deductible of such coverage when the damage is caused by any motor vehicle if the operator of such vehicle has been positively identified and is solely at fault.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to waivers of deductibles in automobile insurance policies.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Motor Vehicle Insurance; Requirements of Coverage.  Amend RSA 412:8, III to read as follows:

\t\tIII. When an automobile insurance policy contains coverage for physical damage by reason of collision, any deductible amount of such coverage shall not apply if the damage is caused by [an uninsured] any motor vehicle, including a motor vehicle which is uninsured within the definition of RSA 259:117, and the operator of the [uninsured] vehicle has been positively identified and is solely at fault.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect January 1, 2023.
