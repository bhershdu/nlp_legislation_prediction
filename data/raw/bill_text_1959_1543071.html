STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to power line maintenance and construction.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Service Equipment of Public Utilities; Installation and Maintenance of Power Lines.  Amend RSA 370:12 to read as follows:

\t370:12 Installation of Power Line Extensions. Any New Hampshire utility customer that requires a power line extension located on private property may hire a contractor who is [licensed in the state of New Hampshire and is] approved by the utility. Such contractor shall supply and install materials specified by the local utility for underground and overhead line extensions, the cost of which shall be borne by the utility customer.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
