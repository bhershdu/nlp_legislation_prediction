SENATE BILL\t23

 

AN ACT\tchanging the requirement that a member of the sponsoring charitable organization be present during certain games of chance.

 

SPONSORS:\tSen. Sherman, Dist 24; Sen. French, Dist 7; Sen. D'Allesandro, Dist 20; Rep. Leishman, Hills. 24

 

COMMITTEE:\tWays and Means

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

AMENDED ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill repeals the requirement that a member of the sponsoring charitable organization be present during games of chance at least once during the contract period.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty One

 

AN ACT\tchanging the requirement that a member of the sponsoring charitable organization be present during certain games of chance.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Presence of Charitable Organization Members; Cross Reference Removed.  Amend RSA 287-D:23, IX(b)(3)-(5) to read as follows:

\t\t\t\t(3) Operating a game on a different date than licensed without approval of the lottery commission; or

\t\t\t\t(4) Committing 3 or more minor violations within 2 years[; or

\t\t\t\t(5) Knowingly operating a game of chance without a representative of the charitable organization present as specified in RSA 287-D:14, XI].

\t2  Repeal.  RSA 287-D:14, XI, relative to the requirement that a member of the sponsoring charitable organization be present during certain games of chance, is repealed.

\t3  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
