AN ACT\trelative to reimbursements for telemedicine.

ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill makes changes to the reimbursement limits for telemedicine.  This bill also further defines telemedicine.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty One

 

AN ACT\trelative to reimbursements for telemedicine.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Medicaid Program Reimbursement.  Amend RSA 167:4-d, III(b) and (c) to read as follows:

\t\t\t(b) The Medicaid program shall provide coverage and reimbursement for health care services provided through tele medicine [on the same basis as the Medicaid program provides coverage and reimbursement for health care services provided in person].

\t\t\t(c) The combined amount of reimbursement that the Medicaid program allows for the compensation to the distant site and the originating site shall [not be less] be no greater than that the total amount allowed for health care services provided in person.

\t2  Medicaid Program Reimbursement.  Amend RSA 167:4-d, III(e) to read as follows:

\t\t\t(e) The Medicaid program shall provide reimbursement for all modes of telehealth, including video and audio, [audio-only,] or other electronic media provided by medical providers to treat all members for all medically necessary services.

\t3  Telemedicine; Definition.  Amend RSA 415-J:2, III to read as follows:

\t\tIII. "Telemedicine," as it pertains to the delivery of health care services, means the use of audio, video, or other electronic media for the purpose of diagnosis, consultation, or treatment. Telemedicine does not include the use of audio-only telephone or facsimile.

\t4  Telemedicine; Insurance Coverage.  Amend RSA 415-J:3, III to read as follows:

\t\tIII. An insurer offering a health plan in this state shall provide coverage and reimbursement for health care services provided through telemedicine [on the same basis as the insurer provides coverage and reimbursement for health care services provided in person].

\t5  Compensation; Limit.  Amend RSA 415-J:3, V to read as follows:

\t\tV. The combined amount of reimbursement that a health benefit plan allows for the compensation to the distant site and the originating site shall be [the same as] no greater than the total amount allowed for health care services provided in person.

\t6  Reimbursement; Telehealth.  Amend RSA 415-J:3, XI to read as follows:
