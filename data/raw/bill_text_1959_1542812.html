AN ACT\trelative to funding for the Piscataqua River turning basin project in the port authority project.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Piscataqua River Turning Basin; Appropriation.  Amend 2021, 107:14, II to read as follows:

\t\tII.  The sum of [$1,500,000] $1,751,143 is hereby appropriated for the biennium ending June 30, 2023 to the Pease development authority for the Piscataqua River Turning Basin.  Of the amount appropriated, $251,143 shall be a charge against the United States Army Corps of Engineering refund back to the state for the Rye Harbor Dredging Project per 2019, 146:1, XIII, B.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect upon its passage.

METHODOLOGY:

This bill increases a previously approved capital budget appropriation by $251,143 relative to the Piscataqua River Turning Basin, which shall be a charge against a United States Army Corps of Engineering refund back to the state.  This bill will have no impact on state, county, and local revenue or expenditures.
