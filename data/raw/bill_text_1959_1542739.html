ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill changes the name of the department of environmental services to the department of environmental protection and authorizes the department to regulate private drinking wells.

METHODOLOGY:

This bill enables the Department of Environmental Services to enter private property to inspect and sample private wells and also changes the name of the “Department of Environmental Services” to the “Department of Environmental Protection” (effective January 1, 2022). The Department states the name change will require the state to expend substantial resources to replace or revise the agency’s name on every aspect of its operation. Costs relative to the name change are indeterminable at this time, however it is assumed all would be incurred in FY 2023.
