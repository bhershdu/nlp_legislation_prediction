AN ACT\trelative to folded ballots.

 

SPONSORS:\tRep. Moffett, Merr. 9

 

COMMITTEE:\tElection Law

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill requires that in any political subdivision that uses electronic ballot counting machines, any ballot that has been folded be held for hand counting after the close of the polls.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to folded ballots.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Elections; Folding and Depositing Ballot.  Amend RSA 659:23, II to read as follows:

\t\tII. The provisions of paragraph I shall not apply in cities and towns which use one or more electronic ballot counting devices for the counting of ballots, as provided in RSA 656:40. In cities and towns which use one or more electronic ballot counting devices for the counting of ballots, any ballot that has been folded after the voter has marked the ballot shall not be counted using such electronic ballot counting devices.  Such ballots shall be set aside and counted by hand in accordance with RSA 659:61 after the close of the polls.

\t2  Processing Absentee Ballots; Depositing Ballot.  Amend RSA 659:52 to read as follows:

\t659:52  Opening Envelope; Depositing Ballot.  If the absentee ballot is not challenged, the moderator shall, after announcing the name of the voter, open the envelope containing the ballot so the affidavit is not destroyed.  The moderator shall then take the ballot out of the envelope without unfolding the ballot or without permitting the ballot to be examined, and he or she shall preserve the affidavit with the ballots cast at the election as provided in RSA 659:101.  The moderator shall then have a checkmark placed beside the name of the absentee voter on the checklist and write therewith the letters "A.V." in red ink and shall then deposit the ballot in the ballot box.  No folded absentee ballot shall be counted using an electronic ballot counting device.  All absentee ballots that have been folded shall be set aside and counted by hand after the close of the polls.

\t3  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
