ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill requires the department of transportation to remove the exit 10 tolls on the F.E. Everett Turnpike in Merrimack.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty One

 

AN ACT\trelative to the tolls at exit 10 in the town of Merrimack.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Department of Transportation; Removal of Toll Booths. The department of transportation shall remove the northbound and southbound toll booths on exit 10 on the F.E. Everett turnpike in Merrimack.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
