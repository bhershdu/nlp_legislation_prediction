STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to solid waste rules and landfill containment tests.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  New Subparagraphs; Solid Waste Working Group; Duties.  Amend RSA 149-M:61, III by inserting after subparagraph (h) the following new subparagraphs:

\t\t\t(i)  Review and make recommendations regarding municipal solid waste plans and implementing the prohibition of any landfill in the state from accepting waste from a municipality that does not have a solid waste management plan.

\t\t\t(j)  Review and make recommendations regarding the development of a solid waste disposal site evaluation committee or better defining alternative site analysis in RSA 149-M:9.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect upon its passage.
