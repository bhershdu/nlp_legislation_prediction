STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty One

 

AN ACT\trelative to sports book locations.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Sports Book Retail Operations.  Amend RSA 287-I:5 to read as follows:

\t287-I:5 Sports Book Retail Operations.  The commission and its agents are further authorized to operate physical sports book retail locations within the state for the purposes of accepting tier I, tier II, and tier III sports wagers from authorized bettors and paying prizes relating to those wagers. The sports book retail locations may be co-located with other commercial businesses or general commercial retail locations.  [No more than 10 sports book retail locations may be in operation at any given time.]

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
