STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

A RESOLUTION\treaffirming support for the nation of Israel and the location of the embassy of the United States therein.

 

\tBE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:

\tThat we reaffirm our strong support for the Jewish people and our truest ally in the Middle East, the Nation of Israel; the recognition of the current and historical capitol of Israel being in Jerusalem; and the location of the embassy of the United States now finally in Jerusalem where it should have been and where it should remain.

\tThat the house clerk is hereby directed to transmit copies of this resolution to the New Hampshire congressional delegation, the Governor of the State of New Hampshire, the United States Secretary of State, and the President of the United States.
