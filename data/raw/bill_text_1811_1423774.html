AMENDED ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill provides that every legislative body, governing body, board, commission, or committee of any village district shall be deemed a “public body” under RSA 91-A and shall be subject to all applicable provisions of that chapter.

AN ACT\trelative to village district public bodies.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  New Section; Village Districts; Public Body.  Amend RSA 52 by inserting after section 11-a the following new section:

\t52:11-b  Public Body.  Every legislative body, governing body, board, commission, or committee of any village district established under this chapter shall be deemed a “public body” under RSA 91-A and shall be subject to all applicable provisions of that chapter.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
