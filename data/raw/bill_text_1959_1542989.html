STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to the guardian ad litem report requirements.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Parental Rights and Responsibilities; Guardian ad Litem.  Report Requirements Modified.  Amend RSA 461-A:16, I-c to read as follows:

\t\tI-c.  The guardian ad litem shall file a report of his or her investigation no later than the date of the final pretrial hearing.  The report shall include only factual information and shall not propose any recommendations [not propose any of the following unless specifically requested by the court:

\t\t\t(a)  An allocation of decision-making responsibility;

\t\t\t(b)  A parenting plan; or

\t\t\t(c)  A specific parenting schedule].

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect January 1, 2023.
