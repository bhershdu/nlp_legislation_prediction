AN ACT\trelative to the authority of the office of professional licensure and certification to establish fees.

 

SPONSORS:\tSen. Giuda, Dist 2; Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Carson, Dist 14; Rep. P. Schmidt, Straf. 19; Rep. Lang, Belk. 4; Rep. McGuire, Merr. 29

 

COMMITTEE:\tWays and Means

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

AMENDED ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill clarifies that the executive director of the office of professional licensure and certification has authority to establish fees on behalf of certain boards, commissions, and councils administered by the office of professional licensure and certification.
