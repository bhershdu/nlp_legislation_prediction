STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty One

 

AN ACT\tauthorizing certain procedures for rulemaking.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Administrative Procedure Act; Definitions.  Amend RSA 541-A:1, V-a to read as follows:

\t\tV-a. "Electronic document" means a document which complies with requirements prescribed by the director for filing under paragraph VI and established in the drafting and procedure manual under RSA 541-A:8. [For electronic documents, a requirement during the rulemaking process for a signature accompanying the filing of an electronic document shall be met if such signature complies with the requirements of RSA 294-E.]

\t2  New Paragraph; Administrative Procedure Act; Definitions; Signature.  Amend RSA 541-A:1 by inserting after paragraph XV the following new paragraph:

\t\tXV-a.  "Signature" means a person's written name used as a form of identification, whether in original, photocopied, or scanned form.

\t3  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
