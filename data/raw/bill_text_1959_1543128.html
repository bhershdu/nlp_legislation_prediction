HOUSE BILL\t1545

 

AN ACT\trelative to a state directory for film production.

 

SPONSORS:\tRep. Wilhelm, Hills. 42; Rep. Egan, Graf. 2; Rep. Dontonville, Graf. 10; Rep. Massimilla, Graf. 1; Rep. Stavis, Graf. 13; Sen. Whitley, Dist 15

 

COMMITTEE:\tExecutive Departments and Administration

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill establishes the duty for the director of economic development to collaborate with the department of natural and cultural resources to establish and maintain a directory relating to film production.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to a state directory for film production.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  New Paragraph; Department of Business and Economic Affairs; Director of Economic Development; Duties; Creation and Maintenance of Film Directory.  Amend RSA 12-O:20 by inserting after paragraph VII the following new paragraph:

\t\tVII-a.  Coordinate with the department of natural and cultural resources to establish and maintain a directory of film and digital production companies, local acting and film-making talent, and filming locations.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
