STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to removing the ramp tolls at exit 11 in Hooksett.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Department of Transportation; Hooksett Tolls Eliminated.  Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, the commissioner of the department of transportation shall eliminate the northbound and southbound ramp tolls for exit 11 on the Everett turnpike in the town of Hooksett.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
