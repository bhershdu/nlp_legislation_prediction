AMENDED ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill revises the child support guideline percentages, increases the self-support reserve amount, defines a shared parenting plan, establishes a new formula for the calculation of child support in cases involving shared parenting plans; and permits the court to modify the presumptive child support amount as justice and the best interest of the child may require.

\t1  Child Support Guidelines; Definition of Self-Support Reserve. Amend RSA 458-C:2, X to read as follows:

\t\tX. "Self-support reserve" means [115] 120 percent of the federal poverty guideline for a single person living alone, as determined annually by the United States Department of Health and Human Services.

\t2 New Paragraph; Child Support Guidelines; Definition of Shared Parenting Plan. Amend RSA 458-C:2 by inserting after paragraph XI the following new paragraph:

\t\tXII. “Shared parenting plan” means a parenting plan where the child overnights with each parent 30 percent or more of the time.

\t4  Child Support Guidelines; Child Support Formula. Amend RSA 458-C:3, II(a) to read as follows:

\t\tII.(a) When there is a shared parenting plan, the total support obligation shall be determined by multiplying the parents' total net income, as defined in RSA 458-C:2, VI, by the appropriate percentage derived from this section and multiplying the resulting number by 1.5. \t5  Child Support Guidelines; Child Support Formula.  Amend RSA 458-C:3, II(d) to read as follows:

\t6  New Paragraph; Adjustments to the Application of the Guidelines Under Special Circumstances; Modification of Child Support As Justice and The Best Interest of the Child May Require.  Amend RSA 458-C:5 by inserting after paragraph II the following new paragraph:
