COMMITTEE:\tEducation

 

-----------------------------------------------------------------

 

ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill clarifies the responsibility of a scholarship organization under the education freedom accounts program for explaining rights under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA).

AN ACT\trelative to participation in the education freedom accounts program by students with disabilities.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Education Freedom Accounts; Responsibilities of the Scholarship Organization; Parents of Students with Disabilities.  Amend RSA 194-B:4, III to read as follows:

\t\tIII.  The scholarship organization shall ensure that parents of students with disabilities receive notice that participation in the EFA program is a parental placement under 20 U.S.C. section [1412] 1412(a)(10)(A), Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA), along with an explanation of the rights that parentally placed students possess and waive under IDEA and any applicable state laws.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect 60 days after its passage.
