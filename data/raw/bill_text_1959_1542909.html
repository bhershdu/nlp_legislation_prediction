ANALYSIS

 

\tThis bill clarifies the governor's duty to provide a list of expenditures in a state of emergency to the executive council.

STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to the governor's duties during a state of emergency.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1  Homeland Security; Governor Duty.  Amend RSA 21-P:43-a to read as follows:

\t21-P:43-a  Governor Duty.  The governor shall provide a list of all expenditures pursuant to this subdivision under a state of emergency to the executive council [for informational purposes only] within 30 days of the expenditure, in order to ensure transparency.

\t2  Effective Date.  This act shall take effect upon its passage.
