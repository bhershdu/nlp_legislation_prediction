STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

A RESOLUTION\texpressing the appreciation of the general court to Nicole Knox Murphy for her song "My 603."

 

\tWhereas, Nicole Knox Murphy is a resident of Candia, New Hampshire; and

\tWhereas Ms. Knox Murphy is a nationally-recognized, multi-award winning singer/songwriter; and

\tWhereas Ms. Knox Murphy was inspired by the natural beauty of New Hampshire to write the song “My 603”; and

\tWhereas Ms. Knox Murphy has dedicated this song to the citizens of New Hampshire; now, therefore, be it

\tResolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:

\tThat the Senate and the House of Representatives hereby and on behalf of all of the people of New Hampshire extend our deepest and heartfelt thanks to Nicole Knox Murphy for sharing her talent and creativity with the people of New Hampshire and in so doing is a credit to herself, her profession, and the great state of New Hampshire.

\tThat the clerk of the house of representatives shall send a signed copy of this resolution to the governor, the members of New Hampshire's congressional delegation, and Ms. Nicole Knox Murphy.
