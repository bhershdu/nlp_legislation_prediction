STATE OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

 

In the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand Twenty Two

 

AN ACT\trelative to reproductive rights.

 

Be it Enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court convened:

 

\t1 Title. This act shall be known and may be cited as the New Hampshire access to abortion-care act.

\t2  New Chapter; Abortion: Rights Established.  Amend RSA by inserting after chapter 132-A the following new chapter:

CHAPTER 132-B

ACCESS TO ABORTION-CARE

\t132-B:1  Access to Abortion-Care.

\t\tI. It shall be the public policy of New Hampshire that, because it is vital to the equality and liberty of all individuals, the state shall not restrict or interfere with an individual's exercise of their private decision to terminate a pregnancy except as provided in RSA 329:44 and RSA 132:32 through RSA 132:36.

\t\tII Enforcement.

\t\t\t(a) An individual injured as a result of a violation of this chapter shall have a private right of action in superior court against the state for injunctive relief arising from the violation.

\t\t\t(b) In addition to any injunctive relief awarded, the court may award costs and reasonable attorney’s fees to an injured person who prevails in an action brought under this chapter.

\t3 Effective Date.  This act shall take effect upon its passage.
